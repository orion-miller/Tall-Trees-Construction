About our Company :

Electro Ohmics is a start-up company aiming to develop, manufacture, and sell nanopore technologies for DNA, RNA, and protein detection.

Our Vision:

At Electro Ohmics, we aim to provide rapid and inexpensive biomarker detection for disease prevention and improved health care outcomes.

Our Mission:

Our goal is to develop this technology to produce a convenient product capable of rapid multiple biomarker detection for hospitals and labs for patients everywhere. ELECTRO-OHMICS’s mission is to use electricity for multiomic (genomics, proteomics, metabolomics,…).
